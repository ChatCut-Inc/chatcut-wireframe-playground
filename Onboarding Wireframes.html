What I'm designing to

Problem: after signup, users land on / (the prompt creator) with 6 scenario cards + a template browser, and don't know what to do.

Goal: get a brand-new user to their first export, fast.

Variation axis: how much we ask before showing value — from zero questions to heavy role+goal pickers.

caveat

What I couldn't verify

I don't have access to your PostHog, AWS DB, or Axiom from here. Every direction has a red assumption callout — the specific data point you should sanity-check before committing.

The wireframes are grounded in the real code: PromptCreator, ScenarioCardGrid, scenario-preset-cards, Better Auth OTP/Google, project-list.

How to read these

  • Each direction starts at the auth moment (signup → land) and ends when the user sees their first real output.
  • Arrows between frames = the user's next click / action.
  • Red margin notes = assumptions that want PostHog evidence.
  • Use the Tweaks panel (toggle in toolbar) to change frame size or hide the assumption callouts for a cleaner read-through.
